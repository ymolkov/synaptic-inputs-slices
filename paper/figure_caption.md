**Figure X.** Synaptic architecture of the rhythmic slice and functional mapping to the intact respiratory network. 
**(A)** Schematic diagram of effective synaptic interconnections between the three key populations: inspiratory inhibitory (VGAT-I), inspiratory excitatory (VgluT2-I), and expiratory inhibitory (VGAT-E) neurons. Arrow thickness reflects the relative strength of synaptic inputs (Strong, Moderate, Weak) derived from conductance measurements. 
**(B)** Conceptual model mapping the slice populations to functional types identified in the intact network *in situ*. The VgluT2-I population represents the prototype for Pre-Inspiratory/Inspiratory (Pre-I/I) neurons. The VGAT-I population corresponds to Early-Inspiratory (Early-I) inhibitory neurons. The VGAT-E population is proposed to combine the functional roles of Post-Inspiratory (Post-I) and Augmenting Expiratory (Aug-E) neurons. VgluT2-E neurons are omitted from this simplified circuit representation.
